Known Issues

  • Only one instance of SonarView may be running at a time. This means you can't use SonarView on two different devices on the same computer at the same time.
  • Ping360 directly connected to a computer on a serial port is not yet supported. In the meantime you can use the Ethernet interface, or route through Companion/BlueOS.
  • App Icons may not appear correctly on Linux
