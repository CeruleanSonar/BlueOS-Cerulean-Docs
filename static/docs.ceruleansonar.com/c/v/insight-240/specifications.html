Receive Effective Beam Width
SonarView renders beams every 0.5 degrees (181 beams spanning the 90 degree image sector). Beamformed 3dB beam width is about 1.3 degrees forward to 1.9 degrees at 45 degree angle.
Maximum Ping Rate
20 Hz up to 30m range, thereafter reducing due to distance and speed of sound to 10 Hz @ 50m, 5 Hz @ 140m

100BaseT Ethernet
Electrical Connection
Single cable with 4 pin JST for data (Blue Robotics standard), plus a wire pair for power. Adapter provided for RJ-45 data.
